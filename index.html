Services

Truffle offers a solution to identify the best tech experts all around the world.

Data collection

Truffle builds and maintains the largest database of tech talent worldwide by tracking developers, designers and other experts online footrpints on thousands of websites, including: Meetup, Github, Stackoverflow, Dribbble, 500px, Behance, Codepen, Twitter, Facebook, About.me, and Linkedin.

Data unification

Once collected, our data is processed, matched and unified in order to create complete profiles of potential hires.

Skimming top experts

From our gigantic database of experts, our algorithms "skims" and ranks the most interesting profiles. For this, we use information coming from thousands of professional websites and interprete information gathered thanks to machine learning.
